The Neurofic Awards Nomination

The Neuroverse Fanfic Awards 2025 have arrived! To celebrate the vast amounts of work, full of effort and talent, that have poured into this community, we will be giving a spotlight to those that excel!

To start, we will have the community nominate a select few works that stands out among the rest in specific categories. After this, we will have the community vote between them, selecting a winner!

To nominate, type in the name of the work you wish to nominate, or leave it blank if you're not sure.

You may nomination multiple works for a single award if you'd like. Just type their names on multiple lines to do so.

Nomination is open [Date] to [Date] in your timezone.

IMPORTANT RULES

You must have read at least 30 Neuroverse works before nominating works. If you do not meet this requirement, please do not submit a nomination.

You may nominate (full or partially) AI generated works, but they will not be up for voting, and instead be listed separately.
